The Problem

Blueprint Bugs Are Invisible Until They Ship

UE5 does not have a static analysis tool for Blueprints. You find bugs by crashing. That costs you hours, weekends, and sometimes entire milestones.

💥

Broken blend weights crash at runtime

A blend weight sum of 1.03 compiles fine but produces physics explosions, T-poses, or silent animation corruption. You will never find this in the editor.

🔄

Circular dependencies are invisible

Blueprint A casts to Blueprint B which casts back to A. No compiler warning. Just random load order failures and 30-second hitches on level load.

⚠️

Skeleton mismatches cause retargeting nightmares

You retarget a montage from one skeleton to another and it "works" in editor. But the bone mapping is wrong and every animation plays from the wrong root.

🕑

Debug nodes and heavy Construction Scripts ship to production

Print String nodes, debug collision boxes, and Construction Scripts running expensive queries. They compile. They package. They tank your frame rate.
